Understanding EV Charging

Charging Basics

Before using evmeri stations, understand the fundamentals:

Charging Levels:

Level 1 (Slow AC Charging):
- Power: 3-7 kW
- Time: 8-12 hours for full charge
- Use: Overnight charging at home
- Connector: Type 2, Schuko

Level 2 (Fast AC Charging):
- Power: 7-22 kW
- Time: 2-4 hours for full charge
- Use: Public charging, workplaces
- Connector: Type 2

Level 3 (Rapid DC Charging):
- Power: 22-350 kW
- Time: 15-60 minutes for 80% charge
- Use: Highway stops, quick top-ups
- Connector: CCS, CHAdeMO

Connector Types in Ethiopia:

Type 2 (Most Common):
- AC charging up to 22 kW
- Compatible with most European EVs
- Standard for new installations

CCS (Combined Charging System):
- DC fast charging up to 350 kW
- Combines Type 2 with DC pins
- Growing network in Ethiopia

CHAdeMO:
- DC fast charging up to 100 kW
- Japanese standard (Nissan, Mitsubishi)
- Limited availability in Ethiopia

Tesla Supercharger:
- Proprietary Tesla connector
- Up to 250 kW charging
- Tesla vehicles only
